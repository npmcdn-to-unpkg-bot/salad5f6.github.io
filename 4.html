THE STRATOCASTER When many people think of an electric guitar, this is the one that leaps to mind. The Stratocaster is an archetypal instrument among the world's most popular guitars and an elegantly versatile creation that is both a musical and cultural touchstone. At its heart, though, the Stratocaster remains a fantastic tool with unmistakable sound and timeless design that have made it the first choice among players worldwide, many of whom have used it to create much of the most important music of our time.
